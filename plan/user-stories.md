** Story 1: Token Deduction Accuracy **
* Being a User, I want my balance to decrease the amount of the stake I chose immediately as i click **SPIN**. Therefore, I can't cheat the system by double clicking.

** Story 2: Win Resolution ** 
* Being a User, I want a clear message that shows if i **Won** or **Lost** in each spins after each reel stop that gives me an idea of how much tokens/currency i lost or gained.

** Story 3: State Persistance **
* Being a User, my balance should be consistent and if i refreshed the page during a spin. It should be the same so i don't lose tokens due to browser issues.

** Story 4: Game Theme Feedback**
* Being a User, I want to see funny braintor messages about "AI Hallucinations" when i lose the game so i'm aware of the game theme being consistent.

** Story 5: Input Handling ** 
* Being a Developer, I want the **SPIN** button to be disabled when the players current balance is lower than the bet to prevent negative balance state.
